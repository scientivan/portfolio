Built Apps and Deep Learning Model to Identify Materials from Waste and Estimate the Price or Penalty to Be Earned (2022)

I built it by trained the pretrained deep learning model called "YoloV4" to detect various material from objects around me. Those materials are paper, plastic, organic material (food waste, leaves, etc.), and glass. Also, the model can detect aruco which is used to determine the scale of the photo. The model is packaged in a form of executable apps and can estimate the total price/penalty that will be earned by the waste disposer.
